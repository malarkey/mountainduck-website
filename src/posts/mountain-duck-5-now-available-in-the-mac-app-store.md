Version 5 is now available in the Mac App Store as a subscription instead of a perpetual licence. If you previously purchased Mountain Duck in the Mac App Store, you can run version 5 with no limitations for up to one year after your purchase date before you need to move to a monthly or yearly subscription.

<img src="/images/posts/mountain-duck-5-now-available-in-the-mac-app-store.png" alt="">

Unfortunately we can no longer distribute version 4 in the Mac App Store, and subscriptions are not supported through the Volume Purchase Program in Apple Business Manager or Apple School Manager.

If you need a different deployment model for teams or managed devices, we recommend switching to the [perpetual licensing option](https://mountainduck.io/) available outside the Mac App Store.
